About Us

Serving Farmers Since 2001

Mahesh & Company, founded by Mukesh Mantri and managed by Atul Mantri, has been the most trusted name in agricultural equipment and services in Aspur, Rajasthan. Located at Dabrawat Complex near Roadways Bus Stand, we are authorized dealers for Shri Ram Fertilizers, Sonalika Thresher, Ganeshraj Thresher, Honda Engines, and more. From hand pumps to flour mills, diesel engines to motor parts - we serve farmers with quality products and honest service.
